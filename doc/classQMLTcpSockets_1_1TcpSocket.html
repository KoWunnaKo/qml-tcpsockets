Detailed Description

QML wrapper for QTcpSocket.

Can be created from a native socket descriptor returned from a TcpServer incoming connection, or can connect to a peer on its own. Due to the sandboxing of WinRT, cannot listen on nor connect to localhost sockets on this platform.
